Cottage Meatloaf Recipe

Cottage meatloaf is a beloved comfort food dish that has been a staple in many households for generations.

This classic recipe is a perfect blend of tender ground meat, flavorful toppings, and a crispy crust, making it a crowd-pleaser for both kids and adults.
In this article, we will guide you through the preparation and cooking process of this mouth-watering dish, ensuring that you achieve a perfectly cooked and delicious cottage meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • Meatloaf:
    • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
    • ¾ cup fresh bread crumbs
    • ½ cup ketchup
    • ⅓ cup tomato juice
    • ¼ cup diced onion
    • 2 large eggs, beaten
    • 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
    • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper

    Topping:

    • ½ cup ketchup
    • 4 teaspoons brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard

    Directions

    1. Step 1

      2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 9x5-inch loaf pan with aluminum foil.

    2. Step 2

      3. Make meatloaf: In a large bowl, mix ground beef, bread crumbs, ketchup, tomato juice, onion, eggs, mustard, salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper together until well combined.
      Press mixture into the prepared pan.

    3. Step 3

      4. Make topping: In a separate bowl, mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together until smooth. Spread mixture over meatloaf.

    4. Step 4

      5. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink, 35 to 45 minutes. Drain off fat. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

    Conclusion

    Cottage meatloaf is a classic comfort food dish that is sure to become a staple in your household.

    With its tender ground meat, flavorful toppings, and crispy crust, this dish is perfect for family gatherings, potlucks, and casual dinner parties.

    Whether you’re a seasoned cook or a beginner, this recipe is easy to follow and requires minimal ingredients. So go ahead, give it a try, and enjoy the delicious results!
