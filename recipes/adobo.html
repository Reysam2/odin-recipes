Adobo Chicken Recipe

Description

Chicken Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish of braised chicken in a sweet and tangy sauce, made with vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves. Usually eaten with rice.

Ingredients

  • Skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce (not dark)
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 small diced brown onion
  • 1 tbsp whole black pepper
  • 2 tbsp sugar (preferably brown)
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 green onion sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup water

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine chicken soy sauce, minced garlic, vinegar, and bay leaves in bowl.. Marinate for at least 20 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet high heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Remove chicken from marinade and place in pan, sear both sides until brown (do not cook chicken all the way through)

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside

  5. Step 5

    6. Heat remaining oil in skillet, then add garlic and onion, cook for 1 1/2 minutes

  6. Step 6

    7. Add reserved marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper

  7. Step 7

    8. Bring it to a simmer, then turn heat to medium high. Simmer 5 minutes.

  8. Step 8

    9. Add chicken smooth side down. Simmer uncovered 20-25 mns. Flip chicken at around 15mns until sauce reduces to jam-like syrup.

  9. Step 9

    10. If sauce isn't thick enough then remove chicken and let sauce simmer by itself

  10. Step 10

    11. Coat chicken in glaze then serve over rice
